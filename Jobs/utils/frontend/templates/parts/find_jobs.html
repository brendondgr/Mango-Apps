Scraping Guide

Enter the main job titles or roles you are looking for. These are the primary terms used to query job boards. Separate terms by pressing Enter.

Specify a city and state (e.g., "Seattle, WA"). If left blank, some sites may default to "Remote". Use the 2-letter state code for best results (e.g. TX instead of Texas).

Filter jobs by how recently they were posted. 24 hours is recommended for finding the most competitive opportunities.

Select which platforms to search. LinkedIn and Indeed usually provide the highest volume of listings.

Use "AND" groups for strict filtering. The title must match at least one keyword from every group.

Group A: "Senior", "Lead"
Group B: "Python", "Node"
Matches "Senior Python Dev"
Fails "Junior Node Dev"

Max results determines how many jobs to fetch per platform. High values combined with LLM refinement will increase total scrap time significantly.
