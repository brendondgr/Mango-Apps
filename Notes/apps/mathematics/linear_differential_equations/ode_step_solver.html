Method Summary

We use the Integrating Factor Method. For the equation \( y' + P(x)y = Q(x) \), we find \( \mu(x) = e^{\int P(x)dx} \). The general solution is then \( y(x) = \frac{1}{\mu(x)} \left[ \int \mu(x)Q(x)dx + C \right] \).
