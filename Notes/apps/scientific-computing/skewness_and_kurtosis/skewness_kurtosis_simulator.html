Skewness

Measures the asymmetry of the probability distribution. In a perfectly symmetric distribution, Mean = Median = Mode. Positive skew pulls the tail to the right, while Negative skew pulls it to the left.

Kurtosis

Measures the "tailedness" of the distribution. Leptokurtic (>0) distributions have heavy tails and a sharp peak. Platykurtic (<0) distributions have thin tails and a flatter peak.
